Our Story

Threaded Dreams was born from a love of fiber art and a desire to create pieces that bring warmth and character to any space. Founded by Maya Chen in her Portland studio, every piece is hand-woven using sustainably sourced natural fibers.

We use only organic cotton, reclaimed wood, and locally sourced dyes. Each piece takes between 8-40 hours to create, ensuring that every knot, loop, and weave is placed with intention and care.
